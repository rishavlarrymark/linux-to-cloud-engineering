DAY 6 — System Services & Scheduling

SSH:
systemctl status ssh
Result: Active and running

Logs:
journalctl -u ssh -n 5
Meaning: SSH running normally

Cron:
* * * * * echo "cron-ok" >> /tmp/cron.log
Meaning: Runs every minute

Timers:
systemctl list-timers
Observed: apt-daily.timer, man-db.timer

Conclusion:
Cron → basic scheduling
systemd timers → reliable OS automation
